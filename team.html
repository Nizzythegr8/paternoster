Our company

Pater Noster Insurance Brokers Limited was incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters ACT 1990 on the 21st March 2002 RC NO. 446189, and registered With National Insurance Commission with registration NO: RBC 1114 under the insurance act 2003. Our company is a firm of Financial Services Experts in Insurance Brokerage, Authorized Share capital of N 10,000,000.00 fully paid.

By virtue of the high standard of our services and the calibre of our clientele, we maintain adequate professional Indemnity Insurance cover. We ensure quality in our service delivery with state of the art information technology and extraordinary human capital committed to the highest standard in our Core Business Areas. Our company no doubt operates as a clearly innovative company applying market led theory.

We are out to provide the best services there is as far as the profession offers to both our clients and our partners. We have a very hard working and result oriented management team, backed up by a visionary board comprising young and agile investors and professionals who have distinguished themselves in their respective field of endeavours.
